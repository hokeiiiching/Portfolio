For future reference: My experience with Bell's Palsy

# Onset
Random jaw cramp while eating subway. The cramp was in a very weird spot; it was in the muscle thats directly at my lower jaw.
Next day: Weird involuntary muscle spasms on the top right side of my face, near my right eye.
Next day: Woke up, face felt a bit tight. Did not really realize what's wrong until I looked in the mirror: I could not smile fully on the right side of my face.

# Symptons:
Tightness on right side of face; unable to smile fully, on the right side of my face. Because I cannot move the right side of my face, it led to my right face feeling very tight and my ear being unable to hear properly.

# Medications and side effects
Prednisolone - An anti-inflammatory steroid to help stop my nerves in the right side of my face from being further damaged. Helps to prevent complete paralysis of the right side of my face.

However, side effects were horrible. Made me unable to sleep while feeling lethargic. Was hungry a lot more often. Cough and sore throat.
Reduced immune system.

# Reflection
On the bright side - I got it very mild, for both Bell's Palsy and the symptons of the medication.
